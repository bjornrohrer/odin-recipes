Bread

Description

For a loaf of crusty Italian bread, try this bread recipe. The dough is made in a bread machine, then the loaves are baked in the oven.

Ingredients

  • 4 Liters of water
  • 100 grams of yeast
  • 160 grams of fine salt
  • 5000 grams of flower

Steps

  1. Pour water into mixing bowl
  2. cut yeast into small pieces and but in mixing bowl
  3. Mix water and yeast until dissolved
  4. Pour in flower and salt into mixing bowl
  5. Mix for 20 on high speed
