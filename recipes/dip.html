Perfect Summer Dip

Description

Perfect dip for your next pool party

Ingredients

  • 1 pint full-fat sour cream
  • 3 to 4 tablespoons granulated sugar, or more to taste
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk all ingredients together until the sugar has dissolved.
  2. Taste the dip, adding more sugar and vanilla if desired.
  3. Serve alongside fresh fruit.
